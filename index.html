Liang Liao (Under Construction)

I am currently working with Prof. Shin'ichi Satoh as a postdoctoral researcher at the National Inititute of Informatics (国立情報学研究所), Japan. Before this, I received my Ph.D degree at National Engineering Research Center for Multimedia Software (国家多媒体软件工程技术研究中心), Wuhan University (武汉大学) with honors in Jun. 2019. I received my B.Eng. degree in the International School of Software, Wuhan University in Jun. 2013. My research interets focus on visual signal coding, restoration and understanding, in particular its applications tp video compression, image inpainting, video frame interpolation, and low-quality image analysis.
